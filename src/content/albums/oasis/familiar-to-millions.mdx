 Familiar to Millions is a live album by the British band Oasis. It was recorded during their performance at London's Wembley Stadium on the 21st of July 2000. It debuted at #5 in the UK charts with 57,000 copies sold in the first week. The album has since gone platinum in the UK and has sold around 70,000 copies in the U.S.A. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Oasis/Familiar+to+Millions">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
